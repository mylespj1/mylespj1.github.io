The Artist

Hi, I'm Samantha Jessop

I'm a passionate artist based in Utah, creating work that celebrates the quiet beauty of the natural world — mountains, wildflowers, temples, and the beautiful people that live in this world!

"And God saw everything that he had made, and, behold, it was very good" - Genesis 1:31

My work is sold at farmers markets, online, and through commissions. I love connecting with people in person — come find me at a market and let's talk art!
